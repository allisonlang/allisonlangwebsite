About Me

I’ve been working as a creative professional for the past 10 years as a film & photo producer within the advertising sector, creating award winning digital, print and OOH ads for my clients and managing creative teams. I have lived in California, New York, Berlin and Barcelona - picking up inspiration along the way.

In 2023, I decided to switch gears and complete a UX / UI Design bootcamp with CareerFoundry, making me well-versed in product development, wire framing and prototyping.

From my advertising photography education and past work experiences, I understand the impact of a simple design which has helped me become a successful designer.
