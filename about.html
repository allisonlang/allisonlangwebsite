About

In the last 10 years, I've been working as a film & photo producer creating award winning digital, print and OOH ads for my clients and managing creative teams. I have lived in California, New York, Berlin and Barcelona - picking up inspiration along the way.

As my passion for creativity grew, I made the transition to UX/UI design where I apply my skills and interests to build aesthetically pleasing designs.

From my advertisement photography education and past work experiences to completing a UX/UI bootcamp, I understand the impact of a simple design as well as strong collaboration which has helped me become a successful creative.
