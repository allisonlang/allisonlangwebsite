My Work

EcoTask

A native mobile app solving ways users can offset their CO2e emissions. The app highlights a smart to-do list that is generated based on the user’s daily activities guiding them toward carbon neutrality.


ClimbToTopo


ClimbToTopo is a mobile app that acts as a digital climbing guidebook and connects climbers around the world to share their knowledge on the best crags.



Gemma


Gemma is an easy-to-navigate online shopping platform consisting of curated second-hand vintage clothing and home goods. It is designed to encourage users to sell and purchase pre-owned pieces - all while making a positive impact on the environment.
